FAQ

How long does it take to setup my server?

Servers are setup automagically within 60 seconds after payment is received. If there are any issues with your server deployment please open a ticket with our support and they will get you sorted out ASAP as you are our top priority.

How many slots am I limited to?

We do not limit our customers to a specifc slot amount. Instead our customers order the amount of ram and cpu power they need and can set the slot count to their desired amount. Selling servers this way allows us to offer you a lower price than our competition and to allow you to only order exactly the resources your need.

Do you offer dedicated IPs?

Yes, we have dedicated IPs available at checkout. If you purchase a dedicated IP please open a ticket request the setup of the address after your payment has processed.

Can I use mods, workshop content, or other custom resources on my server?

Yes you can. We offer several automated tools to get you started but our support staff is standing by to assist you get your server setup exactly how you want it.

Which control panel do you use?

We use an internal maintained version of the open source Pterodactyl Panel which we heavly modify to make it easier for our cusotmers to utilize and automate many functions of operating a game server.

Can I use my own save/map?

Yes. if you have an existing map you want to use it can easily be uploaded to your server using our file manager.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my server?

Yes. You can change your plan/resources at any time and your server will automatically have resources added or removed based on your choice.

Do you provide DDoS protection?

Yes, of course! Our automated systems tackle the brunt of these attackers but if you find yourself under attack let our staff know and they will make sure additional mitigators are put in place for your server.
